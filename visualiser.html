Audio visualiser

I created this Audio Visualiser as a piece of coursework for my first year module introduction to programing. it uses a range of techniques to create four animations that are manipulated by a piece of audio.

Spin

Spin takes a piece of audio and analises a range within the audible sound spectrum and separates this range into 1024 bins. the volume of each of these bins and moves the shape on screen accordingly. The animation is then rotated around the center of the screen.


Wave Pattern

Wave Pattern analysis a piece of audio using the waveform to manipulate three lines and three concentric circles.

Spectrum

Spectrum takes both the spectrum and waveform analysis creating three different animations. These animations appear at the point of a mouse click and move in a random direction and speed.

Video

Video uses the webcam functionality and manipulate the colour of the image using the spectrum analysis.

Bio

I am currently in my first year of the BSc Computer Science Degree course at Goldsmiths College, University of London. I have always held an interest in computing and technology from an early age and am now hoping to develop this into a career.
Within the last year I have been studying the fundamental concepts that govern computing in tandem with the mathematical theories behind it. on top of this I have also been learning to implement these ideas through coding in the javascript language as well as website design using html and css.
On this site you will find a collection of my work from the past year this will include an Audio Visualiser that I created for the module Introduction to Programming, an application Sudoku I created for problem solving for Computer Science and an essay on the application shazam i wrote for fundamentals of Computer Science.
